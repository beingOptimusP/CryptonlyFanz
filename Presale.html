How does the presale work?
Our presale contract will instantly transfer your SYMP Tokens directly to your Trustwallet or Metamask wallet.

Metamask Guide

Step 1:
Download Metamask mobile app or chrome extension.

Step 2:
Setup BSC on your Metamask.
You can follow this tutorial

Step 3:
Transfer your BNB from an exchange to your Metamask wallet. (BEP20)

Step 4:
Decide the amount of BNB to send based on our terms above.

Step 5:
When sending, change the "Gas Limit" amount to 299999.

Step 6:
Send BNB to the SYMP Token Presale Contract address:

0xCb31101105fbE82D5f18D4CcE84e914Bf628F45E

Step 7:
Once your transaction is confirmed, our Presale Contract will instantly send your SYMP Tokens to your Metamask wallet which your BNB was sent from.

Step 8:
In your Metamask wallet click on custom token and add the SYMP Token contract address

0x8801638949eaD3B5bF8dBB2E2AAdadCc2fB011de

You may need to enter the following details if they are not automatically generated
Symbol: SYMP
Decimals: 18
