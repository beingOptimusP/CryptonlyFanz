WHAT IS SYMP Token?

As the cryptocurrency of the SYMP.FANS platform, SYMP Token (SYMP) will incentivize influencers and their fans by providing additional benefits and enhanced user engagement.
As the SYMP.FANS user base grows so will the demand for SYMP leading to an active token economy from which all holders will benefit.

What makes SYMP unique?

LEGITIMATE USE CASE

SYMP will be used throughout our SYMP.FANS social network

LOW COST & HIGH SPEED

SYMP is a BEP-20 token built on the Binance Smart Chain which is one of the fastest blockchain technologies providing lowest fees

6% TOTAL Tax

Buy and hold SYMP and you will automatically get more in your wallet. No need for farming or staking.

2% TO HOLDERS

The more our SYMP.FANS network expands the more often SYMP will be used throughout the site, resulting in a continuous distribution to your wallet.

2% TO LIQUIDITY

By distributing 2% back to the SYMP liquidity pool we aim to keep the market liquid, the liquidity pool balanced, and the price stable.

2% TO SYMP FUND

SYMP Fund aims to give back to our community every month in the form of SYMP giveaways and more! This will be a transparent and community driven fund that will allow our community to have a say in how the SYMP Fund is allocated.

There will be various options the community will vote on such as: SYMP giveaways, Charitable donations, SYMP burn to lower total supply, Competition rewards, Fast track feature requests and many more!
